What is Hacking?

Hacking Is the activity of hacking and breaking into a security system,Hacking activity is also referred to as the process of identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities in a system or network to gain unauthorized access to system data and resources.It can also be defined as unauthorized intrusion into information systems/networks by attackers at the expense of security.Hacking Example: Exploiting the weakness of the default password to gain access to the data stored in the system.
